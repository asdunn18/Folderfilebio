Adam Dunn

More to Know

Adam has worked for the Spartanburg County Public Libraries since 2019. He served as a Community Connections Assistant, Public Information specialist and the Audio, and Music Specialist for the Headquarters branch. He also served as a web developer. In this role, he was responsible for updating the public events calendar and troubleshooting links and functionality of the public website.

Over the past 15 years, Adam has crossed through many hobbies, careers, and interestes. Starting as a fast food server, continuing on to a degree in music performance, side stepping as a privite cook at a country club, continuing to work with high school bands, working two years in elementry music, and finally meeting the love of my life and sharing a passion for books. While music is still a very important part of his life, books have become a way of life.
